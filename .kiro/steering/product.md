# Product

This is the website for the Department of Information Technologies (Кафедра ИТ) at Bryansk State Engineering-Technology University (БГИТУ), Russia.

**Live site:** http://kit.hostedu.ru/

## Purpose

A single-page informational site presenting the history of the IT department from 1972 to 2012. It displays:
- A year-by-year timeline of department events
- Graduate lists per year (with thesis titles and VK social links)
- A photo carousel of graduating classes
- Links to the official university page and the department's VK community

## Audience

Students, faculty, and alumni of the БГИТУ IT department. Content is in Russian.

## Scope

Currently a read-only, data-driven history page. There is no admin panel, user authentication, or user-generated content.
